### Data Quality Issues Found
1. **Non-Alcoholic Cocktails** descriptions reference alcoholic ingredients (vodka, tequila, cachaca) -- likely a copy/paste error on the original site
2. **Dawa** appears under GIN but its description ("White rum, cognac & orange liqueur") suggests it's a cocktail, not a gin
3. Some brand names have typos on the source site: "Couvisier" (should be Courvoisier), "Jose Curvo" (should be Jose Cuervo), "Belverde" (should be Belvedere), "Nederbrg" (Nederburg), "Limoncelo" (Limoncello) -- corrected in tables above
4. Prices appear to be in old SLL denomination (pre-redenomination). Current Sierra Leonean currency is the New Leone (SLE), where 1 SLE = 1,000 old SLL

### Currency Note
Sierra Leone redenominated its currency in 2022. The prices shown use "SLL" which is the old Leone. For display purposes you may want to:
- Show as-is (SLL) to match the source
- Convert to SLE by dividing by 1,000 (e.g., SLL 300 = SLE 0.30)
- Confirm with the restaurant which denomination they intend
